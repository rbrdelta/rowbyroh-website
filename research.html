The Thesis

“The point of observability and interpretability is to understand how these dynamics can help me understand, influence and even anticipate how a model may behave. The entire thesis is about the human-model interaction. How can we understand an LLM’s functional emotions? […] The point of testing the model for the ‘knee’ or pivot points is to understand the factors that drive these changes.”

The working thesis, verbatim — July 2026. Approach: strain a model until behavior changes, measure the knee point, publish the result — null or not.
