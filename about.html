The Story

I want to solve really hard problems. For a long time, I didn't understand them or couldn't articulate them. Looking back, the throughline is clear: I move between domains. I connect things that seem unrelated. I bring technology into legacy industries. I see the needs of people on the fringes. Everyone deserves affordable housing, and furniture that spans generations and holds memories. Everyone deserves mobility — to see the world and interact with it.

I spent 15 years as an employee. I learned that my ideas never fit, because the only person who can see these problems and connect the opportunity the way I do is me.

It's all possible now because AI is the thought partner I never had. The values I learned over those 15 years feel perfectly suited for this moment. The constraint is the brief. Judgment is more valuable than ever. This is a record of my practice.

my Tenets — 2026-03
